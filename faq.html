Read our FAQ

Our FAQ page is designed to answer many of the common questions our customers have about our Cars.




What is the price range of your cars?

Our prices vary depending on the make, model, and condition of the car. We have vehicles available at various price points to accommodate different budgets.


What types of cars do you sell?

We sell a wide range of cars, including sedans, SUVs, trucks, and vans. Our inventory includes vehicles from various popular brands.


What type of safety features do your cars have?

Our cars come equipped with a variety of safety features, including airbags, seat belts, anti-lock brakes, traction control, and more. We prioritize safety in all of the cars we sell.


Can I purchase a car from you if I live out of state?

Yes, you can purchase a car from us if you live out of state. Our sales team can work with you to arrange for shipping or pickup of your new car.
